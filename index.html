Poet or Philosopher?

If you stopped to think for one second, this book is for you.

Reusable Cross

Irony of religious spin

by Davor Banovic

Thought provocative and mind-boggling read on author's own quest through republic of letters to essence of Christianity. By exploring ideas accros fields, from philosophy, natural sciences and Bible to movies, poetry and popular music, Banovic is rediscovering the power of dialogue and so needed civilized discourse in matters that survive over time and space... Matters like personal faith and religious spin.

A thrilling book debut.
